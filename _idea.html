Idea

The idea to make Skillshelv.es was born after Fluidinfo imported a large proportion of all books ever published by O'Reilly Media into their FluidDB and offered developers a prize if they come up with an exciting application for the writable API. My idea is pretty simple: You list which O'Reilly books are in your bookshelf and skillshelv.es analyzes your bookshelf to find out what skills you have.

That's the idea behind skillshelv.es. If you want to know more you should sign up and build your own skillshelf. You could also check out my blog where I intend to write up some more details about this project once this thing is fully operational.
